South Beach Hotel Kiten

South Beach hotel was built in a romantic resort at the Black Sea-Kiten Resort. It is situated near one of the central street, and is only about 400m from the beach. The hotel offers its guests a variety of accommodation: two single rooms, nine double rooms, four three-bed rooms, and five apartments. The rooms are equipped with magnetic locking cards, air-conditioner, TV, fridge and Internet. South Beach hotel has a large panoramic terrace. The hotel offers to its guests rent-a-bike and rent-a-car services and organizes horse riding tours, tours to other resorts in the district and to the local natural and historical sight.
